--- title: Creating "soft-links" to folders on Linux date: 2008-11-11T01:00:00.000Z comments: true categories: - Linux url: /blog/2008/11/creating-soft-links-to-folders-on-linux type: post --- If you're working on Linux, especially on the command line, you'll often have to navigate through a lot of directories. This clearly depends on your directory structure, but it may be the case. That's however really annoying, so creating a link may easy your life:
ln -s <fileonharddisk> <name>
